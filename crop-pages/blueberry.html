Blueberry

Blueberries are small, round berries known for their sweet-tart flavor and deep blue color. Native to North America, they thrive in acidic soils and are prized for their antioxidant-rich properties. Blueberries are used in baked goods, jams, and smoothies, and can be eaten fresh. They grow on bushes and are a favorite among both home gardeners and commercial farmers.
